Operational Risk & Remediation Dashboards

This project demonstrates how data can be used both to identify operational risks and to design actionable remediation strategies.

It includes two complementary dashboards designed to monitor backlog risk and track document remediation performance.

KRI Dashboard (Risk Monitoring)

KRI (Key Risk Indicator)

The KRI dashboard provides a comprehensive view of backlog volumes over the past 12 months. Trend indicators highlight increases and decreases over time.

The As-of Date table compares snapshots across multiple timeframes (1 day, 30 days, 90 days, 1 year). Increases in backlog are considered undesirable and are highlighted in red, while decreases are shown in green.

This dashboard is designed to support biweekly operational meetings by allowing teams to quickly focus on critical risk areas instead of reviewing all data.

Document Remediation Dashboard

This is a hypothetical dashboard created with dummy data to prepare for a client engagement before project kickoff.

The scenario focuses on transferring documents to a new third-party provider within a one-year deadline. The dashboard evaluates whether the current transfer rate is sufficient to reduce the backlog to zero.

If the current pace is not enough, the dashboard estimates the additional transfer capacity required to meet the target, enabling proactive planning and decision-making.
